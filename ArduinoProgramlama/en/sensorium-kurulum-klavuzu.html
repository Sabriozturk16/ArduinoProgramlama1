Installation Guide
Step 1

Check the product box for existing hardware.

Step 2

Stick the double-sided tapes to the back side of the device.

Step 3

Non-stick surface is removed from the double-sided tape.

Step 4

After the non-stick surface is removed, carefully mount the device to the nearest location.

Step 5

Find an appropriate location within the fridge for the temperature probe.


Step 6

Carefully mount the temperature probe to the surface with double-sided tape.


Step 7

Carefully fix the fridge door open/close probe to the firdge door.


Step 8

Attach the fridge door open/close probe to the related socket.



Step 9

Attach the cable to the intermediate socket.


Step 10

Attach the other end of the cable to the device.


Step 11

Attach the ethernet cable to the related socket.


Step 12

Attach the other end of the ethernet cable to your modem or switch. Your internet connection will be established.

Step 13

Connect the AC/DC adaptor to the device.


Step 14

Put the memory card to the SD card slot.


Step 15

Connect the AC/DC adaptor to electricity.


Step 16

Check all the connections.
